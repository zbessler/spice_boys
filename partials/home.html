OUR WINES

Hellloo?
Pale yellow in color, with ripe citrus notes and juicy tropical aromas. Fresh acidity with bright lemon and grapefruit flavors which are ripe and refreshing on the palate. Crisp and delightful with a smooth, medium-body and elegant finish.
Production area: Valle del Rapel
Vineyard name: Casablanca Valley, Chile
Soil composition: Granitic with red clay, sandy loam and
Elevation: 900 to 1300 feet
Varietal composition: 100% Sauvignon Blanc
Length of aging before bottling: 4 months on the lees
Alcohol: 13.0%
pH level: 3.3
Acidity: 6.5 g/L
